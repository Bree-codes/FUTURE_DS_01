# 🚀Internship Track – Data Science & Analytics

## Future Interns – Business Sales Performance Analytics (FUTURE_DS_01)

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to uncover key insights on revenue trends, product performance and regional sales distribution. The goal is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that support data driven business decisions and strategy.

## Objectives
Analyze revenue trends over time to identify growth patterns
Identify top-performing products and categories driving revenue
Evaluate regional sales performance and market opportunities
Provide actionable business recommendations based on findings

## Tools & Technologies Used
Programming & Analysis: Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
Dashboarding & Visualization: Streamlit, Plotly
Environment: Pycharm IDE

## Analysis Performed
Data cleaning, preprocessing, and validation
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify patterns and anomalies
Sales trend analysis over months and regions
Evaluation of product and category performance
Regional revenue comparison and contribution analysis

## Executive Summary
Total Revenue: $8,911,407.90

## Business Recommendations
Increase inventory and marketing efforts for top-performing products.
Expand operations in high performing regions to maximize revenue.
Review underperforming products and adjust pricing, promotion, or distribution strategies.
Monitor monthly sales trends to identify growth opportunities and seasonal patterns.

## Conclusion
The analysis identifies key revenue drivers and growth opportunities. By focusing on high performing products and regions, the business can optimize its operations and improve overall performance. The interactive Streamlit dashboard provides stakeholders with real time insights for informed decision-making.
